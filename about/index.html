About

isthispersonaweso.me was founded on the love of fun. The idea came to mind when I was viewing isbukkitupdated.com made by lol768. Namecheap.com had a deal for $1 .me domains so I thought why not buy one and make a site like this. And so I did.

That is how this site came to be
